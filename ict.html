About (ICT)

Information and Communications Technology (ICT) is technology that is used to handle communications processes such as telecommunications, broadcast media, intelligent building management systems, audiovisual processing and transmission systems, and network-based control and monitoring functions.



In a very simplified sense, ICT is used to talk about all of the technologies that facilitate communications. With that in mind, the term ICT has had several differing definitions applied to it. It's generally associated with consolidating and managing telecom infrastructure and converging technologies that have common transmission lines.

ICT Exams in LFKES

1. It Olympiad

I.T. Olympiad is India's first ever online and championship on a national level for students from class III to class XII. This is the 11th year of the championship and more than 6,00,000 students have participated so far.

2. CCC [Course On Computer Concept]

CCC Exam 2021 – The National Institute of Electronics and Information Technology (NIELIT) conducts the Course on Computer Concepts (CCC) exam throughout the year. The exam will be held in online mode. The candidates wishing to pursue a course on computer concepts (CCC) need to apply online. The officials commence the registration on the first of every month. The aspirants must check the eligibility criteria before filling the application form to avoid rejection. The officials will end the application process on 30/31st of the month.
